Selected Work

Projects

Mathematical Modelling of Nurse Macrophage-Driven Erythropoiesis Disruption in AML

A computational and systems biology study of AML-driven disruption of erythropoiesis, integrating ODE-based modelling, sensitivity analysis, and biologically grounded hypothesis generation.

Full Thesis Abstract Model Repo GSA Repo

BioLionaire Scientist: Volume 6 – Code Empire

A co-authored volume exploring computational biology, bioinformatics, genomics, artificial intelligence applications, and biotechnology innovation and business strategy.

SIR Epidemic Simulation (EVD in West Africa)

A Julia-based simulation of Ebola Virus Disease spread in West Africa using the SIR model, with interactive parameter tuning, epidemic curves, and GIF animation.

Peritoneal Metastasis Prediction

Transcriptomics + ML for biomarker panels and peritoneal metastasis prediction in gastric cancer; external GEO validation.

Single-cell Pipelines

5-miRNA Signature for Early Detection of Gastric Cancer
